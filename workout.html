Workout

Workout is the best way to keep your body fit and healthy. Here are some of the workout plans that you can follow.

Click start on the button the workout for the Workout Level.

Beginner


Beginner workout plan is designed for those who are new to the gym and want to start their fitness journey. It provides a basic introduction to exercise and focuses on building a foundation of strength, flexibility, and cardiovascular endurance. The beginner plan typically includes simple exercises with lighter weights, as well as guidance on proper form and technique. It is aimed at helping individuals develop confidence and familiarity with the gym environment while gradually improving their fitness level..



Intermediate


Intermediate workout plan is an intermediate workout plan is a fitness program designed for individuals who have surpassed the beginner level of fitness and are looking to further challenge themselves. It typically involves a combination of cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and flexibility work.




Advanced


Advanced workout plan is designed for individuals who have a solid foundation of strength, endurance, and overall fitness. It is intended for those who have been consistently working out for a considerable amount of time and are looking to further push their limits. The advanced plan incorporates complex exercises, advanced training techniques, and higher intensity workouts to challenge individuals and help them achieve their fitness goals at a more advanced level.
